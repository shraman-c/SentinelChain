Project Goals
1. Primary Goal: Instant Tamper Detection
Build a lightweight private blockchain that secures SIEM logs and immediately detects unauthorized modifications, deletions, or insertions.

2. Evaluation Goal: Measure Detection Latency
Quantify the system's effectiveness by calculating the exact time difference (in milliseconds or nanoseconds) between a database tamper event and the system's automated alert.

3. Architectural Goal: High Throughput, Low Overhead
Ensure the system can process thousands of incoming logs per second without the computational bloat of traditional public blockchains (no mining, no complex consensus).
